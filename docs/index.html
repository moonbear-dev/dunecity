DuneCity

Dune II meets SimCity — build your empire on Arrakis

DuneCity is a hybrid RTS + city-builder. The classic Dune II engine with full SimCity-style city simulation integrated. Place residential, commercial, and industrial zones, build roads, manage budgets, and defend your city from sandworms and enemy forces. The spice must flow — and so must the tax revenue.

Dune Legacy

Original engine

DuneCity is a fork of Dune Legacy, an open-source recreation of Dune II. If you want the classic Dune II experience without city-building, grab Dune Legacy from SourceForge.

  • SourceForge
  • Earlier releases

Requirements

Dune II data files required. DuneCity needs the original Dune II PAK files to run (DUNE.PAK, SCENARIO.PAK, etc.). You must legally own a copy of Dune II: The Building of a Dynasty. The game will prompt you to locate these files on first launch.
